About us:

Hello everyone! We are Grade 11 students from Santa Laurensia who participated in an educational trip (Edutrip) to Japan. Through this website, we aim to share our experiences exploring the educational and cultural beauty of Japan. During our journey, we visited various places that not only offered stunning views but also provided meaningful learning opportunities. From historical landmarks to modern innovations, each destination gave us new insights and perspectives. We hope this website can inspire others to see travel not only as leisure, but also as a valuable educational experience.
